```jsx
<Headline size={1}>
  Headline 1
</Headline>
<Headline size={2}>
  Headline 2
</Headline>
<Headline size={3}>
  Headline 3
</Headline>
<Headline size={4}>
  Headline 4
</Headline>
<Headline size={5}>
  Headline 5
</Headline>
```

In case, you want to adjust font-size without semantic emphasis, you can specify the underlying tag.

```jsx
<Headline tag="span" size={1}>
  Headline 1
</Headline>
```
